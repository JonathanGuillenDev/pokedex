Pokédex for Red, Blue, and Yellow

"Pokémon Red", "Pokémon Blue", and "Pokémon Yellow: Pikachu Edition" are 1996-98 video games for the Nintendo Game Boy. They are the first installments of the Pokémon video game series. Below is information pulled live from https://pokeapi.co, the RESTful Pokémon API. The data from each response is represented as a card. This Pokédex consists of the first 150 (plus 1 secret) Pokémon available in the games.
